日本 Japan

2024 · 32 张

大阪、京都、奈良，原本可以被写成一串清晰的地名。但对我们家来说，日本还有一层很具体的吸引：我们都是任天堂的粉丝。于是这趟旅行里，寺庙、鹿、街道、游乐园和那些熟悉的游戏符号自然混在一起。大人想记住城市和路线，孩子记住真正让自己开心的东西。我们都很喜欢日本，也期待下一次能慢下来，体验得更多一点。

Osaka, Kyoto, and Nara could have been written as a neat line of place names. But for our family, Japan carries another very specific pull: we are all Nintendo fans. So temples, deer, streets, amusement parks, and those familiar game-world signs naturally folded into the same trip. Adults try to remember cities and routes; children remember what truly made them happy. We all loved Japan, and I hope next time we can slow down and experience more of it.
