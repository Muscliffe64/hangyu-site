关于子峪 About Ziyu

2025-2026 · 170 张

关于子峪不是一趟远行，而是一年多里反复经过的日常：学校、山路、苞谷地、晚霞、冬天的雪、教室里的孩子、屋前屋后的树影。照片越来越多，时间也被它们悄悄分成许多小段。它们没有一个明确的目的地，却让我更清楚地看见陪读生活的纹理：孩子在长大，山下的季节在变，而我试着把这些普通日子认真留住。

About Ziyu is not a journey far away, but a daily path I kept passing through for more than a year: school, mountain roads, cornfields, sunset clouds, winter snow, children in classrooms, and tree shadows around the house. As the photographs accumulated, time quietly broke into smaller pieces. They do not point toward a clear destination, but they let me see the texture of this life of accompanying a child through school: the child grows, the seasons below the mountains change, and I try to keep these ordinary days with care.
