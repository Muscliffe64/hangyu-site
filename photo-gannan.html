甘南 Gannan

2023 · 中国 · 甘肃南部 · 12 张

甘南的照片并不多，却像把高原的几个瞬间留了下来：航拍里弯曲的山路，草坡上细小的人，夜空里的银河，和一束花前安静的脸。这里的山不像景区宣传里那样明亮，它有风、有雨、有长时间的沉默。很多时候，我拍下的不是壮丽本身，而是人在壮丽面前短暂停住的样子。那种停顿，比目的地更像旅行。

There are not many photographs from Gannan, but they hold a few clear moments from the plateau: winding mountain roads from above, small human figures on grass slopes, the Milky Way at night, and a quiet face before a bunch of flowers. The mountains here are not as bright as travel posters make them seem. They have wind, rain, and long silences. Often I was not photographing grandeur itself, but the way a person briefly pauses before it. That pause feels more like travel than the destination.
