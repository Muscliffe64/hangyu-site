影像 Films

这些年陆续做的纪录与影像创作。远行、自然、岛屿与孩子们的成长时刻，每个片子都是一段慢慢展开的对话。

Documentary and film works made across recent years: journeys, nature, islands, and the small luminous moments of growing up.

我的南极奥德赛 My Antarctic Odyssey

2024.02.05 · Antarctic Peninsula · 37:55 · 4K

从穿越德雷克海峡开始，跨越两万公里抵达南极半岛。冰川、企鹅、海豹，以及那种近乎宗教的寂静，一路把身体的不适和心里的震动都留在画面里。

A journey from the southern tip of South America across the Drake Passage to the Antarctic Peninsula. Ice, penguins, seals, seasickness, and a silence that feels almost sacred.
