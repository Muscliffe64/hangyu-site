爪哇 Java

2025 · 印度尼西亚 · 婆罗浮屠 + 默拉皮 · 42 张

在爪哇，时间不是被放在博物馆里的。它还留在街道、寺庙、火山和人们的日常里。日惹的夜路、婆罗浮屠的石阶、默拉皮的暗火，还有司机 Wibi 递来的信任，把这趟旅行连成一条线。很多照片看起来是在拍风景或街头，其实是在拍一种缓慢发生的连接：从火山到长安，从一叠印尼盾到比特币，从陌生人到朋友。

In Java, time is not sealed inside museums. It remains in streets, temples, volcanoes, and ordinary days. The night roads of Yogyakarta, the stone steps of Borobudur, the dark glow of Merapi, and the trust offered by Wibi the driver joined this trip into one line. Many of these photographs seem to be about streets or landscapes, but to me they are about a slow connection: from a volcano to Chang'an, from a stack of rupiah to Bitcoin, from strangers to friends.
