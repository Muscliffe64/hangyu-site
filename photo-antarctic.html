南极半岛 Antarctic Peninsula

2023 · 36 张

从乌斯怀亚出发，跨过德雷克海峡，南极半岛像一片被风和冰反复擦亮的边界。那里一切都显得过于安静：船头前的海、雪坡上细小的人影、独自站着的企鹅、远处的浮冰。可真正靠近它之后，又会发现这种安静并不空白，而是带着巨大的重量。有些地方，抵达一次之后，就很难真正离开。我很确定，这不会是我唯一一次来到这里。

Leaving Ushuaia and crossing the Drake Passage, the Antarctic Peninsula appeared like a boundary polished again and again by wind and ice. Everything there seemed almost too quiet: the sea before the bow, tiny figures on snow slopes, a penguin standing alone, icebergs in the distance. Yet once I came close, that quietness was not empty. It carried weight. Some places are difficult to truly leave after you reach them once. I am quite sure this will not be my only time here.
