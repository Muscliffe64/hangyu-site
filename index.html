Hangyu Ji is a documentary filmmaker, photographer, writer, and lecturer in Recording Arts at Xi'an Conservatory of Music. In 2008, he entered the Recording Arts program at Communication University of China; in 2013, he continued his studies in Sound Production for Film and Television at Bournemouth University in the UK. In 2016, he founded Xi'an Everything Film & Culture Media Co., Ltd. He continues to make documentaries while exploring how AI and blockchain can enter traditional arts education.
